About Krystene

A Journey of Healing & Discovery

Krystene Du Maurier is a remote healer, cross-over medium, and psychic intuitive renowned for her expertise in Kundalini Energy Transmission, spirit rescue facilitation, and clairvoyant guidance. Drawing on decades of experience, she empowers individuals on their spiritual awakening and transformative journeys through remote and in-person sessions, channeling universal energy to promote deep healing, clarity, and personal growth.

Krystene's compassionate approach supports clients in overcoming emotional, mental, and spiritual blockages, bridging the realms of the living and the spirit world with sensitivity and respect. Her unique blend of energy work, mediumship, and intuitive insight offers a safe, nurturing space for holistic well-being, spiritual advancement, and meaningful self-discovery.

Her Philosophy

Reconnecting with Your Divine Nature

At the heart of Krystene Du Maurier's work lies a profound philosophy: the recognition that every soul's journey is a chapter in a cosmic tapestry of interwoven narratives — my story, your story, our collective story. She believes Divinity is not remote or abstract, but actively at play in human form and expression, revealing itself through our struggles, triumphs, and even the subtle moments of transformation. Krystene sees her role as a facilitator not only of healing but of awakening to the sacred interplay between the individual and the universal spirit and Source. By guiding others through their personal stories and connecting them to the collective threads of humanity, she invites clients to witness the unfolding dance of Divinity within themselves and each other — a process where healing, clarity, and growth become not just possibilities, but living realities. Through this creative approach, Krystene encourages each person to embrace their path as a sacred expression, trusting that the journey itself is Divine in its never-ending unfolding and becoming.
