About

Krystene Du Maurier

Kundalini Energy Transmission

Guiding Spiritual Awakening and Healing Through Universal Energy

Krystene is a skilled Kundalini Energy transmission facilitator who specializes in remote healing sessions designed to support and empower individuals throughout their personal awakening and transformational journeys. Drawing from decades of experience and a deep connection to universal energy, Krystene offers a unique healing practice that transcends physical boundaries — her sessions can be experienced in person, over the phone, FaceTime, or via Zoom, each equally powerful and tailored to the recipient's needs.

At the core of Krystene's practice is Kundalini Energy Transmission — a process in which kundalini energy, often referred to as "raw life force energy," is transferred from the facilitator to the recipient. Krystene acts as a clear channel for this universal energy, never utilizing her own personal energy. Instead, she allows this powerful force to activate and flow through her hands, gaze and intention. This transmission activates the recipient's energy field and inner landscape, sparking deep healing, spiritual awakening, and lasting transformation. The experience is often likened to lighting one candle from another; once Krystene's kundalini energy is awakened and flowing, she ignites that spark within others, guiding them gently to a new awareness of their inner energy.

In each session, Krystene orchestrates the flow of Kundalini energy in "waves," supporting the integration of your Higher Self and fostering unconditional love through the heart chakra. The process is facilitated by Source, with every wave building upon the last, allowing time for reflection, connection, and sharing. Her nurturing presence ensures a safe, supportive environment where clients can relax, receive, and embrace their unique journey of self-discovery. Throughout the session, recipients may experience sensations such as gentle vibrations, warmth, tingling, or profound stillness — each a sign of the energy working to promote healing and growth.

Krystene's role extends beyond channeling energy; she serves as a compassionate guide, helping clients recognize and heal unconscious suffering, clear deep-seated emotional, mental, and spiritual blockages, and expand their consciousness. Her work is rooted in the belief that Kundalini Energy Transmission can fast-track spiritual growth and reinforce one's connection to their innate divinity and the collective whole. Whether you are new to energy healing or seeking deeper spiritual advancement, Krystene's sessions offer a transformative space to awaken your own energy, restore balance, and embark on a meaningful path of holistic well-being.

Cross-Over Medium & Spirit Rescue Facilitator

Spirit Rescue Facilitation

Bridging Worlds for Healing, Closure, and Spiritual Transformation

Krystene is a gifted cross-over medium and spirit rescue facilitator, recognized for her compassionate and clear ability to bridge the physical and spiritual realms. Since childhood, she has attracted non-transitioned, or "earthbound," spirits seeking her guidance to return to the light or Source. Often, these spirits result from sudden or unexpected deaths, making the transition difficult to process. Additionally, certain religious or cultural beliefs — especially those depicting the afterlife in binary terms like heaven or hell — can sometimes cause confusion or hesitation for souls moving forward. Krystene addresses these situations with sensitivity and respect, honoring all perspectives and providing support to both her clients and the spirits, helping them find healing, closure, and peace.

As a spirit rescue medium, Krystene specializes in helping lost or stuck spirits find their path home by providing a "bridge" of light, energy, and communication. Through both mental and physical mediumship, she acts as a conduit — sometimes using her own energy to facilitate safe passage for spirits — while always maintaining clear boundaries and a nurturing presence.

Krystene often works with loved ones connected to earthbound spirits, helping families find resolution, comfort, healing on both a physical and cellular level, and closure. Her gentle, supportive approach remains focused on the well-being of her clients. If you are grieving a loss or experiencing unexplained fatigue, disrupted sleep, mental fog, reduced joy, or unusual cold sensations, Krystene offers compassionate consultations to determine if energetic clearing or spirit release may bring relief.

Her sessions empower individuals on their journey of awakening and transformation, fostering spiritual growth, healing, and renewed balance. Krystene's work as a cross-over medium is both a calling and a gift, providing a bridge between worlds to restore harmony for both the living and those who have yet to fully transition.

Clairvoyant Psychic Intuitive Guide

Clairvoyant Guidance

Insightful Guidance for Personal and Spiritual Clarity

With over two decades of experience as a healer and psychic intuitive, Krystene offers deeply immersive, heart-centered sessions that draw upon her innate sixth sense and finely tuned extrasensory perception. When you book an intuitive session with Krystene, you are welcomed into a compassionate space where she connects with your unique energetic signature and the underlying currents shaping your life. Her psychic abilities allow her to access insights about your relationships, environment, ambitions, self-expression, passions, and the intentions of your higher self. Krystene's goal is to bring clarity, assist in identifying the right actions, and address your questions and concerns with empathy and accuracy.

In addition to her psychic gifts, Krystene's deep background in music and sound healing enhances her intuitive work, giving her the ability to perceive the subtle harmonics and resonance fields present in your life. This unique skill set enables her to tune into both the overarching themes of your soul's journey and the specific steps needed in your present situation. With visionary perspective, she serves as a guide who bridges the broad scope of your spiritual path with practical, actionable guidance, supporting you in moving forward on your journey to wholeness and self-realization.
